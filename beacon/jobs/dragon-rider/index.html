    Limit Break: Dragon Echo
    • Action: Minor Action
    • Range: Range = Scope
    • Tags: Summon
    • Specific Type: Limit Break

    Dragon Echo ( Size 3, {HP} = Grit+15, Dodge/ A-Def 10, Speed 6) You summon an echo of your dragon within Range. This dragon has flight and can move up to its SPEED during your turn. Allies can share spaces with it, and any allies fully within its spaces can move with it when it moves. The dragon lasts until the end of the scene or until defeated. The dragon can also attack with the Echo Wave weapon as a standard action during your turn, adding your GRIT to the attack roll.

    Echo Wave Melee Weapon Heavy Artifact Special [{Close Blast} 2] | [3 {Astral} + 1 {Discord}]

    Create two SIZE 1 jump pads in free spaces within Range. When you or an ally enter a jump pad's space, you (or they) immediately fly up to 5 spaces, ignoring reactions. Jump pads are destroyed after they are used, and using this ability again destroys all old pads. If not destroyed, they last until the end of the scene.
