    Wind Spells

    Razor Wind

    Razor Wind

    Tier: Cantrip | 1 Action

    Range: 12 | Target: Single Target | Damage: 1d4 slashing (Vicious)

    Vicious: roll 1 additional die whenever you roll crit damage. Also damages up to 1 adjacent target.


    High Levels: +2 damage every 5 levels.
    Note: Songweaver Only.

    Breath of Life

    Breath of Life

    Tier: Cantrip | 1 Action

    Range: 6 | Target: Single Target

    Restore 1 HP to a Dying creature.


    High Levels: +2 Range every 5 levels.

    Blustery Gale

    Blustery Gale

    Tier: 1 | 2 Actions

    Range: 12 | Target: Single Target | Damage: 3d4 bludgeoning

    Advantage against flying, Small, or Tiny targets. On hit: Move a Med target 2 spaces away; Small/Tiny twice as far; Large half as far (round down). For each die you would roll due to forced movement from this spell, deal +5 damage instead.

    Upcast: +1 movement.

    Barrier of Wind

    Barrier of Wind

    Tier: 2 | 1 Action

    Range: N/A | Target: Self

    Ranged attacks have disadvantage against you this round (including the triggering attack).

    Upcast: +3 Armor.

    Fly

    Fly

    Tier: 3 | 1 Action

    Range: N/A | Target: Single Target+

    | Concentration: Up to 10 minutes

    Touch a creature, grant a flying speed of 12.

    Upcast: +1 target.

    Eye of the Storm

    Eye of the Storm

    Tier: 4 | 2 Actions

    Range: Reach: 3 | Target: AoE | Damage: 4d4+10 bludgeoning

    Damage to enemies within Reach. You may place surviving creatures anywhere within 1 space of the storm's Reach on a failed STR save.

    Upcast: +1 Reach.

    Updraft

    Updraft

    Tier: 5 | 3 Actions

    Range: Reach: 12 | Target: AoE

    Enemies within a 5x5 area must repeat a DEX save until they succeed. For each time they failed they suffer 1d6 falling damage and land prone.

    Upcast: +2 Range, +1 area.

    Thousand Cuts

    Thousand Cuts

    Tier: 6 | 3 Actions

    Range: 12 | Target: AoE | Damage: d44 slashing (roll with advantage)

    Also damages enemies within Reach 1 of your target.

    Upcast: +1 Reach.


    Note: D44 with advantage: Roll 3d4 and drop the lowest die. The leftmost die is the tens place, and the second is the ones.

    Boisterous Winds

    Boisterous Winds

    Tier: 7 | 2 Actions

    Range: Reach 12 | Target: Multi-target

    | Concentration: Up to 1 minute

    You and up to 12 allies within Reach gain: Ranged attacks have disadvantage against you, a flying speed of 12, and can move for free 1/round.

    Upcast: +1 minute or +2 targets.

    Vicious Mockery

    Vicious Mockery

    Tier: Cantrip | 1 Action

    Range: 12 | Target: Single Target | Damage: 1d4+INT psychic (ignoring armor)

    On hit: the target is Taunted during their next turn.


    High Levels: +2 damage every 5 levels.

    Wind Whisper

    Wind Whisper

    Tier: Cantrip | 1 Action

    Range: N/A | Target: Single Target

    You whisper a message into the wind and it will be secretly carried to a specified target within 100 miles/160 km.

    Helpful Gust

    Helpful Gust

    Tier: Cantrip | 1 Action

    Range: Reach: 6 | Target: Single Target

    Gently move a Tiny unheld item within Reach in any direction. OR: Generate an illusory scent.

    Feather Fall

    Feather Fall

    Tier: Cantrip | 1 Action

    Range: Reach: 6 | Target: Single Target

    Cause them to gently float to the ground, unharmed.
