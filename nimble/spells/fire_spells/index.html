    Fire Spells

    Flame Dart

    Flame Dart

    Tier: Cantrip | 1 Action

    Range: 8 | Target: Single Target | Damage: 1d10

    On crit: Smoldering.


    High Levels: +5 damage every 5 levels.
    Note: Smoldering: This condition does nothing on its own, though some spells and abilities have additional effects against Smoldering creatures.

    Pyroclasm

    Pyroclasm

    Tier: 4 | 2 Actions

    Range: Reach: 3 | Target: AoE | Damage: 2d20+10 (ignoring armor)

    Others within Reach take damage on a failed DEX save. Half damage on save. Smoldering creatures fail.

    Upcast: +1 Reach, +2 damage.


    Note: Defend: If you've already Defended this round, you can't use abilities requiring it again (even for free).

    Heart's Fire

    Heart's Fire

    Tier: Cantrip | 1 Action

    Range: 4 | Target: Single Target

    Give an ally within Range an extra action. Spend 1 mana to cast this when it is not your turn.


    High Levels: +1 Range every 5 levels.

    Ignite

    Ignite

    Tier: 1 | 2 Actions

    Range: 8 | Target: Single Target | Damage: 4d10 to a Smoldering target

    Ends the Smoldering condition on hit.

    Upcast: +10 damage.

    Enchant Weapon

    Enchant Weapon

    Tier: 2 | 1 Action

    Range: N/A | Target: Single Target

    | Concentration: Up to 1 minute

    A weapon you touch is enchanted with magical flame. It deals +KEY damage and inflicts Smoldering on crit.

    Upcast: +KEY damage.

    Flame Barrier

    Flame Barrier

    Tier: 3 | 1 Action

    Range: N/A | Target: Self

    Until the start of your next turn, melee attackers against you take KEY damage (ignoring armor) and gain Smoldering.

    Upcast: +KEY damage.

    Fiery Embrace

    Fiery Embrace

    Tier: 5 | 2 Actions

    Range: Reach: 8 | Target: AoE

    | Concentration: Up to 1 minute

    While within Reach: 1 ally gains the effects of Enchant Weapon. Enemies gain Smoldering, lose damage resistance, and their damage immunity is reduced to resistance.

    Upcast: +1 ally.

    Living Inferno

    Living Inferno

    Tier: 7 | 3 Actions

    Range: N/A | Target: Self

    Gain the effects of Flame Barrier until your next turn. At the end of this turn and your next turn, cast Pyroclasm for free.

    Upcast: Upcast Flame Barrier and Pyroclasm.

    Dragonform

    Dragonform

    Tier: 9 | 5 Actions

    Range: N/A | Target: Self

    Transform into a Huge dragon. Gain 3 actions, a fly speed of 12, LVL Armor, 10xLVL temp HP, and: Tooth & Claw (Action, Reach 2, 1d20+LVL damage ignoring armor, inflicts Smoldering) and Immolating Breath (2 Actions, Reach: Cone 8, DC 20 DEX save, KEY d20 damage, half on save, Smoldering targets fail). You can maintain this form for as long as the temp HP granted by this spell remain (max. 10 minutes). When it ends, you drop to 0 HP.

    Firebrand

    Firebrand

    Tier: Cantrip | 1 Action

    Range: N/A | Target: N/A

    Touch a surface and secretly mark it with a symbol or brief message. Speaking a chosen command word while nearby reveals it.

    Fire Step

    Fire Step

    Tier: Cantrip | 1 min

    Range: N/A | Target: Self

    Teleport to a fire source you can see.

    Kindle

    Kindle

    Tier: Cantrip | 1 Action

    Range: N/A | Target: Single Target

    Conjure a minor visual illusion. OR: Ignite a small, unheld item within Range 6.
