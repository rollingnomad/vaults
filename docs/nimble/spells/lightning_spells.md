# Lightning Spells

#### Zap

!!! abstract "Zap"
    **Tier: Cantrip** | **1 Action**
    
    **Range:** `12` | **Target:** `Single Target`
     | **Damage:** `2d8`
    

    ---

    **On miss: the lightning fails to find ground, and strikes you instead.**

    ---

    
    <br>**High Levels:** +6 damage every 5 levels.
    

#### Overload

!!! abstract "Overload"
    **Tier: Cantrip** | **1 Action**
    
    **Range:** `Reach: 2` | **Target:** `AoE`
     | **Damage:** `2d8 to others within Reach`
    

    ---

    **Castable only if you are Charged, ending the condition.**

    ---

    
    <br>**High Levels:** +4 damage every 5 levels.
    <br>**Note:** *Charged: Whenever you take lightning damage, you are Charged for 1 minute.*

#### Arc Lightning

!!! abstract "Arc Lightning"
    **Tier: 1** | **2 Actions**
    
    **Range:** `12` | **Target:** `Single Target`
     | **Damage:** `3d8`
    

    ---

    **The bolt also damages the next closest creature to your target. On miss: the lightning fails to find ground and strikes you instead.**

    ---

    **Upcast:** +4 damage.
    
    <br>**Note:** *Next Closest: If you or an ally is the next closest, they are hit! If 2 creatures are equally close, the GM can roll for it or select the one wearing the most metal.*

#### Alacrity

!!! abstract "Alacrity"
    **Tier: 2** | **1 Action**
    
    **Range:** `4` | **Target:** `Self`
    
    

    ---

    **After damage is dealt, you gain the Charged condition then teleport anywhere within Range.**

    ---

    **Upcast:** +4 Range.
    
    

#### Stormlash

!!! abstract "Stormlash"
    **Tier: 3** | **2 Actions**
    
    **Range:** `Line: 12` | **Target:** `AoE`
     | **Damage:** `3d8+4 (ignoring metal armor)`
    

    ---

    **Surviving creatures are Dazed on a failed STR save, or Incapacitated instead for 1 of their turns if they fail by 5 or more. Creatures with a large amount of metal (e.g., armor or a longsword) roll with disadvantage.**

    ---

    **Upcast:** +4 damage.
    
    

#### Electrickery

!!! abstract "Electrickery"
    **Tier: 4** | **3 Actions**
    
    **Range:** `8` | **Target:** `2 Targets`
    
    

    ---

    **Choose another creature within Range to swap places with your ally on a failed WIL save (they become the new target). Costs 1 Action while Charged, ending the condition.**

    ---

    **Upcast:** +2 Range.
    
    

#### Electrocharge

!!! abstract "Electrocharge"
    **Tier: 5** | **2 Actions**
    
    **Range:** `N/A` | **Target:** `Single Target`
    
     | **Concentration:** `Up to 1 minute`

    ---

    **A creature you touch gains the Charged condition, +1 max action, +5 armor, 2x speed, and advantage on DEX saves.**

    ---

    **Upcast:** +4 Range.
    
    

#### Ride the Lightning

!!! abstract "Ride the Lightning"
    **Tier: 6** | **3 Actions**
    
    **Range:** `N/A` | **Target:** `AoE`
     | **Damage:** `d88`
    

    ---

    **Teleport up to 12 spaces away to a spot you can see (if a willing creature is there, swap places with them). Adjacent creatures take damage. Surviving creatures must make a STR save or be hurled back 3 spaces, knocked Prone, and deafened for 1 day.**

    ---

    **Upcast:** +1 DC.
    
    <br>**Note:** *D88: Roll 2d8. The leftmost die is the tens place, and the second is the ones.*

#### Seething Storm

!!! abstract "Seething Storm"
    **Tier: 9** | **3 Actions**
    
    **Range:** `Reach: 4` | **Target:** `AoE`
    
     | **Concentration:** `Up to 1 minute`

    ---

    **You become a cloud of tempestuous storm. You can fly, move for free 1/round, and attacks against you are made with disadvantage. At the end of each of your turns, strike up to 4 creatures within Reach with a bolt of lightning for d88 damage (a creature can only be struck 1/round). +2 Reach and number of bolts each round. Costs 3 actions each round to maintain. Once you cast this spell, you must Safe Rest for 1 week before you can use it again.**

    ---

    
    
    

#### Spark Buddy

!!! abstract "Spark Buddy"
    **Tier: Cantrip** | **1 min**
    
    **Range:** `N/A` | **Target:** `N/A`
