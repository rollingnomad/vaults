# Fire Spells

#### Flame Dart

!!! abstract "Flame Dart"
    **Tier: Cantrip** | **1 Action**
    
    **Range:** `8` | **Target:** `Single Target`
     | **Damage:** `1d10`
    

    ---

    **On crit: Smoldering.**

    ---

    
    <br>**High Levels:** +5 damage every 5 levels.
    <br>**Note:** *Smoldering: This condition does nothing on its own, though some spells and abilities have additional effects against Smoldering creatures.*

#### Pyroclasm

!!! abstract "Pyroclasm"
    **Tier: 4** | **2 Actions**
    
    **Range:** `Reach: 3` | **Target:** `AoE`
     | **Damage:** `2d20+10 (ignoring armor)`
    

    ---

    **Others within Reach take damage on a failed DEX save. Half damage on save. Smoldering creatures fail.**

    ---

    **Upcast:** +1 Reach, +2 damage.
    
    <br>**Note:** *Defend: If you've already Defended this round, you can't use abilities requiring it again (even for free).*

#### Fiery Embrace

!!! abstract "Fiery Embrace"
    **Tier: 5** | **2 Actions**
    
    **Range:** `Reach: 8` | **Target:** `AoE`
    
     | **Concentration:** `Up to 1 minute`

    ---

    **While within Reach: 1 ally gains the effects of Enchant Weapon. Enemies gain Smoldering, lose damage resistance, and their damage immunity is reduced to resistance.**

    ---

    **Upcast:** +1 ally.
    
    

#### Living Inferno

!!! abstract "Living Inferno"
    **Tier: 7** | **3 Actions**
    
    **Range:** `N/A` | **Target:** `Self`
    
    

    ---

    **Gain the effects of Flame Barrier until your next turn. At the end of this turn and your next turn, cast Pyroclasm for free.**

    ---

    **Upcast:** Upcast Flame Barrier and Pyroclasm.
    
    

#### Dragonform

!!! abstract "Dragonform"
    **Tier: 9** | **5 Actions**
    
    **Range:** `N/A` | **Target:** `Self`
    
    

    ---

    **Transform into a Huge dragon. Gain 3 actions, a fly speed of 12, LVL Armor, 10xLVL temp HP, and: Tooth & Claw (Action, Reach 2, 1d20+LVL damage ignoring armor, inflicts Smoldering) and Immolating Breath (2 Actions, Reach: Cone 8, DC 20 DEX save, KEY d20 damage, half on save, Smoldering targets fail). You can maintain this form for as long as the temp HP granted by this spell remain (max. 10 minutes). When it ends, you drop to 0 HP.**

    ---
