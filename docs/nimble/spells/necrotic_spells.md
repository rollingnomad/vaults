# Necrotic Spells

#### Entice

!!! abstract "Entice"
    **Tier: Cantrip** | **1 Action**
    
    **Range:** `8` | **Target:** `Single Target`
     | **Damage:** `1d4 (ignoring armor)`
    

    ---

    **On hit: target moves 2 spaces closer to you.**

    ---

    
    <br>**High Levels:** Increment the die size 1 step every 5 levels (d6 » d8 » d10 » d12).
    

#### Withering Touch

!!! abstract "Withering Touch"
    **Tier: Cantrip** | **1 Action**
    
    **Range:** `Reach: 1` | **Target:** `Single Target`
     | **Damage:** `1d12`
    

    ---

    **On hit: Target is considered undead for 1 round.**

    ---

    
    <br>**High Levels:** +6 damage every 5 levels.
    

#### Shadow Trap

!!! abstract "Shadow Trap"
    **Tier: 1** | **2 Actions**
    
    **Range:** `N/A` | **Target:** `Single Target`
    
     | **Concentration:** `Up to 1 minute`

    ---

    **The next creature to move adjacent to you suffers 3d12 damage; if Small or Tiny, it is also Restrained by shadowy tendrils for as long as you maintain concentration or until they escape.**

    ---

    **Upcast:** +1 size category, +1d12 damage when they escape.
    
    

#### Dread Visage

!!! abstract "Dread Visage"
    **Tier: 2** | **1 Action**
    
    **Range:** `N/A` | **Target:** `Self`
    
    

    ---

    **Melee attackers are Frightened of you and suffer 1d12 damage if they attack you this round. Costs 2 mana less while dying.**

    ---

    **Upcast:** +2 damage, +2 armor.
    
    

#### Vampiric Greed

!!! abstract "Vampiric Greed"
    **Tier: 3** | **2 Actions**
    
    **Range:** `N/A` | **Target:** `AoE`
     | **Damage:** `4d12 to all adjacent creatures`
    

    ---

    **Gain 1 Wound. Heal HP equal to the damage done. Any surviving creatures make a STR save. Gain 1 additional Wound for each creature that saves.**

    ---

    **Upcast:** +1 DC.
    
    

#### Greater Shadow

!!! abstract "Greater Shadow"
    **Tier: 4** | **2 Actions**
    
    **Range:** `N/A` | **Target:** `N/A`
    
    

    ---

    **Summon a 5d12 Greater Shadow minion (max 1) adjacent to you. When it dies, it explodes into 5 shadow minions (see Summon Shadow). Place them anywhere within 8 spaces.**

    ---

    **Upcast:** +1d12 damage, +1 shadow minion on explosion.
    
    

#### Gangrenous Burst

!!! abstract "Gangrenous Burst"
    **Tier: 5** | **2 Actions**
    
    **Range:** `Reach: Up to 8` | **Target:** `AoE`
     | **Damage:** `3d20 (ignoring armor)`
    

    ---

    **Other damaged creatures must make a STR save or take damage, half on save. The save is rolled with disadvantage while Bloodied.**

    ---

    **Upcast:** +10 damage.
    
    

#### Unspeakable Word

!!! abstract "Unspeakable Word"
    **Tier: 6** | **2 Actions**
    
    **Range:** `8` | **Target:** `Special`
     | **Damage:** `d66 (with advantage, ignoring armor)`
    

    ---

    **On a failed INT save. Does not miss or crit. Target rolls with disadvantage if Bloodied or Frightened. On a success, you both take half of this damage instead.**

    ---

    **Upcast:** +1 DC, +10 damage.
    
    <br>**Note:** *D66 with advantage: Roll 3d6 and drop the lowest die.*

#### Creeping Death

!!! abstract "Creeping Death"
    **Tier: 7** | **3 Actions**
    
    **Range:** `8` | **Target:** `AoE`
     | **Damage:** `4d20`
    

    ---

    **If this kills the creature, it violently erupts and you MUST deal the same amount of damage to another creature within 8 spaces of it that has not yet been damaged by this effect. Repeat until a creature survives this damage or no other creatures are within Reach.**

    ---

    **Upcast:** +1d20 damage.

#### Summon Shadow

!!! abstract "Summon Shadow"
    **Tier: Cantrip** | **1 Action**
    
    **Range:** `N/A` | **Target:** `N/A`
    
    

    ---

    **Summon a shadow minion within Reach 1 (max of INT or LVL minions). Minions have 1 HP, no damage bonus, and do not crit. They abandon you immediately outside of combat. Action: (1/turn) command ALL minions to move up to 6 then attack (Reach 1, d12 each).**

    ---

    
    <br>**High Levels:** +1 Reach every 5 levels.
    <br>**Note:** *Shadowmancer Only.*
