# Wind Spells

#### Razor Wind

!!! abstract "Razor Wind"
    **Tier: Cantrip** | **1 Action**
    
    **Range:** `12` | **Target:** `Single Target`
     | **Damage:** `1d4 slashing (Vicious)`
    

    ---

    **Vicious: roll 1 additional die whenever you roll crit damage. Also damages up to 1 adjacent target.**

    ---

    
    <br>**High Levels:** +2 damage every 5 levels.
    <br>**Note:** *Songweaver Only.*

#### Blustery Gale

!!! abstract "Blustery Gale"
    **Tier: 1** | **2 Actions**
    
    **Range:** `12` | **Target:** `Single Target`
     | **Damage:** `3d4 bludgeoning`
    

    ---

    **Advantage against flying, Small, or Tiny targets. On hit: Move a Med target 2 spaces away; Small/Tiny twice as far; Large half as far (round down). For each die you would roll due to forced movement from this spell, deal +5 damage instead.**

    ---

    **Upcast:** +1 movement.

#### Eye of the Storm

!!! abstract "Eye of the Storm"
    **Tier: 4** | **2 Actions**
    
    **Range:** `Reach: 3` | **Target:** `AoE`
     | **Damage:** `4d4+10 bludgeoning`
    

    ---

    **Damage to enemies within Reach. You may place surviving creatures anywhere within 1 space of the storm's Reach on a failed STR save.**

    ---

    **Upcast:** +1 Reach.
    
    

#### Updraft

!!! abstract "Updraft"
    **Tier: 5** | **3 Actions**
    
    **Range:** `Reach: 12` | **Target:** `AoE`
    
    

    ---

    **Enemies within a 5x5 area must repeat a DEX save until they succeed. For each time they failed they suffer 1d6 falling damage and land prone.**

    ---

    **Upcast:** +2 Range, +1 area.
    
    

#### Thousand Cuts

!!! abstract "Thousand Cuts"
    **Tier: 6** | **3 Actions**
    
    **Range:** `12` | **Target:** `AoE`
     | **Damage:** `d44 slashing (roll with advantage)`
    

    ---

    **Also damages enemies within Reach 1 of your target.**

    ---

    **Upcast:** +1 Reach.
    
    <br>**Note:** *D44 with advantage: Roll 3d4 and drop the lowest die. The leftmost die is the tens place, and the second is the ones.*

#### Boisterous Winds

!!! abstract "Boisterous Winds"
    **Tier: 7** | **2 Actions**
    
    **Range:** `Reach 12` | **Target:** `Multi-target`
    
     | **Concentration:** `Up to 1 minute`

    ---

    **You and up to 12 allies within Reach gain: Ranged attacks have disadvantage against you, a flying speed of 12, and can move for free 1/round.**

    ---

    **Upcast:** +1 minute or +2 targets.
