??? warning "**Trait: Gale Sheath**"
    - **Origin:** Drifter
    - **Job Rank:** 0
    - **Ability Type:** Trait
    - **Specific Ability Type:** Job Trait
    
    
    
    
    - **Action:** Minor Action
    - **Range:** 
    
    - **Tags:** 

    You can sheathe a LIGHT or MAIN weapon that has Reach, imbuing it with the power of wind. One weapon can be sheathed at a time, and once sheathed, you can't attack with it that turn. You gain the following benefits while you have a weapon sheathed:
    - The sheathed weapon gains +1 Reach.
    - When you crit with the sheathed weapon, you can immediately sheathe it after the attack resolves without spending an action.
    - 1/turn, you can deal +1d6 bonus damage on a hit with the sheathed weapon against a target within its Reach.
    These effects end after you attack with the sheathed weapon or sheathe a different weapon. You may choose to start each combat scene with a weapon sheathed.
