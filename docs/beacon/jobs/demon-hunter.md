??? warning "**Trait: Corruption**"
    - **Origin:** Demon Hunter
    - **Job Rank:** 0
    - **Ability Type:** Trait
    - **Specific Ability Type:** Job Trait
    
    
    
    
    - **Action:** 
    - **Range:** 
    
    - **Tags:** 

    You have a Corruption Die, a d6 that starts at 1. When you take {Stress}, increase the Corruption Die by 1, to a maximum of 6. You gain the following effects when you meet their level of Corruption:
    - 2 Corruption: You deal +1 bonus {Force} on hits with attacks.
    - 4 Corruption: Your bonus {Force} increases to +2.
    - 6 Corruption: You can activate your Demon Form trait.



??? warning "**Trait: Demon Form**"
    - **Origin:** Demon Hunter
    - **Job Rank:** 0
    - **Ability Type:** Trait
    - **Specific Ability Type:** Job Trait
    
    
    
    
    - **Action:** Free Action
    - **Range:** 
    
    - **Tags:** Polymorph

    At 6 Corruption, you can reset your Corruption Die to 1 and assume a demonic form until the start of your next turn. While in this form, you gain the following benefits:
    - Your bonus damage from Corruption increases to +3 {Force}.
    - All bonus damage you deal becomes {Discord}.
    - You gain resistance to {Stress} caused by other characters.
    - You have Flight.
    - You gain +1 ARMOR.
