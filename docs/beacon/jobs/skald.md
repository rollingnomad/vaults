## Limit Break


??? danger "**Limit Break: Ballad**"
    - **Action:** Free Action
    - **Range:** Range = Scope
    - **Tags:** Arcing
    - **Specific Type:** Limit Break
    
    You begin weaving a legendary performance. Upon activation and at the start of each of your turns, pick a verse from those listed below. It affects you and all allies within Range. Each verse can only be chosen once.
    Instead of picking a verse (or when there are no more verses to select), you may select a finale from those listed below. It affects you and all allies within Range, following which this ability ends.
    This ability ends at the beginning of your next turn after the finale.
    If you are SILENCED, the effects of all verses are suppressed, and you don't choose any new verses or a finale until you have cleared the condition.
    Verses:
    - Verse of Heroes: +1 {Accuracy} on attack rolls.
    - Verse of Journeys: +2 SPEED.
    - Verse of Relaxation: Gain resistance to {Stress}.
    - Verse of Teamwork: +1 {Accuracy} on all checks and saves.
    Finale:
    - The Last Stand: Gain 1d6+4 FOCUS.
    - The Storm Fades: Regain all MP.
    - Legends Never Die: Regain all {HP}.
    - A New Beginning: Clear all {Stress} and conditions.
    - The New World: Each character can shift up to their SPEED.
