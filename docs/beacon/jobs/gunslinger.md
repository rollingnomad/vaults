## Limit Break


??? danger "**Limit Break: Customized Ammo**"
    - **Action:** Free Action
    - **Range:** 
    - **Tags:** 
    - **Specific Type:** Limit Break
    
    You gain access to the following special ammunition types for the rest of the scene. Each ammunition type can be used once.
    You can choose to us an ammunition type when attacking with a ranged weapon, giving the listed effects. The attack's normal effects still apply.
    - Hotshot: Draw a straight line between yourself and your target, and place a [{Blast} 2] AOE centered anywhere along this line. Characters in the AOE must make an AGILITY save. Failure: They take [2d6 {Astral}]. Success: They take [1d6 {Astral}].
    Additionally, the area becomes dangerous terrain (AGILITY, [5 {Astral}]) until the end of the scene
    - Mindjack Round: On Hit: Until the end of the target's next turn or until they are dealt damage by you or one of your allies, they consider you and your allies as their allies, and their former allies as enemies. On Miss: The target is unable to target you with hostile effects until the end of their next turn.
    - Piercer: The attack gains Piercing and becomes a {Line} attack with a distance of any amount up to the attack's {Range}. The attack also has Reliable 10 against objects and terrain.
    - Shock Round: On Hit: The target is STUNNED and SLOWED until the end of their next turn. On Miss: The target is SLOWED and can't take reactions until the end of their next turn.
