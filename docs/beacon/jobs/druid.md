## Limit Break


??? danger "**Limit Break: Tempest Storm**"
    - **Action:** (2) Channel
    - **Range:** Range = Scope
    - **Tags:** 
    - **Specific Type:** Limit Break
    
    You begin channeling a furious storm, and all adjacent characters of your choice are pushed 1 space away from you. If you stop channeling this ability before releasing for any reason, you do not expend your limit break.
    On Release: Create a 4 x 4 storm zone within Range. The storm lasts until the end of the scene and its spaces are difficult terrain.
    Characters within the storm when it is created or who enter one of its spaces take [1d6+4 {Physical}], no more than 1/turn per character. If a character ends their turn in one of the storm's spaces, you may slide them up to 3 spaces.
    Minor Action: You can spend 1 MP to move the storm up to 3 spaces in a straight line.
