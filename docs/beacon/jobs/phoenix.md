## Limit Break


??? danger "**Limit Break: Supervolcano**"
    - **Action:** (2) Channel
    - **Range:** Range = Scope
    - **Tags:** Homing,  Mana 2
    - **Specific Type:** Limit Break
    
    If you stop channeling this ability before releasing it for any reason, you do not expend the limit break.
    On Release: You create a SIZE 3 volcano - a piece of terrain - within Range. The volcano destroys any objects (including terrain) other than objectives in its spaces. Characters in these spaces are considered to be atop the volcano.
    Mark 6 spaces as lava flows. These must be adjacent to either the volcano or another lava flow space. Lava flows are dangerous terrain (AGILITY, 5 {Discord}).
    Characters within or adjacent to the volcano or a lava flow space receive +2 {Difficulty} on checks to clear {Discord}.
    On subsequent turns, you can spend 1 MP as a minor action to add or remove up to 3 lava flow spaces.
    The volcano and lava flows last until the end of the scene.
