## Limit Break


??? danger "**Limit Break: Perfect Form**"
    - **Action:** 
    - **Range:** 
    - **Tags:** 
    - **Specific Type:** Limit Break
    
    You gain up to four of the following benefits, which last until the end of the scene. 1/turn as a free action, you can change up to four of your chosen benefits. Only one instance of each option can be in effect at a time.
    - Adaptive Body: You gain +1 {Accuracy} on all checks and saves.
    - Dire Beast Weapons: Your Beast Weapons deal 1d6+2 damage instead of their standard damage.
    - Eagle Eye: You can {3} MARK as a minor action.
    - Perfected Form: Form for the Occasion no longer causes {Stress} when you fly, and you have flight.
    - Hunter's Eye: At the start of your turn, you gain 1 Focus.
    - Insulated Hide: You gain resistance to {Physical}, {Astral}, {Lunar}, {Force}, {Stress}, or {Discord} from other characters.
    - Predator Weapons: You gain +1 {Accuracy} on attacks.
    - Resized: You become any SIZE from 1/2 to 4. Characters are pushed to make space for your new size.
    - Sneaky: You can {4} HIDE as a minor action.
    - Walking Wall: You count as hard cover for your allies.
