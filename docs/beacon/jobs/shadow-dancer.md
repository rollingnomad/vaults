    - **Action:** 
    - **Range:**

    Whenever you teleport, you leave a shadow clone in your former space. These clones are the same SIZE as you, don't cause obstruction, and aren't characters or objects.
    1/round when you or an ally damage a target adjacent to a shadow clone, the shadow clone can cause the ability in question to deal +1d6 bonus damage.
